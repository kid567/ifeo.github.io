RC Car

RC Car Preliminary Circuitry

Designed and implemented circuitry for a RC car, integrating essential components like motors (stepper and DC), RF modules, buttons and a joystick. Successfully developed a power distribution system to optimize energy efficiency and ensure reliable performance. Troubleshot and resolved electrical issues, ensuring smooth communication between the remote control and the car.

Triboelectric Nanogenerator

Materials and Electrial Engineering

Developed a triboelectric nanogenerator (TENG) using recyclable and cost-effective materials to harness frictional mechanical energy for small scale power generation. Designed and fabricated the TENG with a focus on material selection to maximize energy output while minimizing environmental impact. Conducted thorough testing and optimization to enhance electrical performance and durability. Demonstrated the device's potential for powering low-energy small scale electronics, showcasing innovative use of renewable materials and advanced energy-harvesting techniques.

3D Printing

Design and Production

Utilized a Creality Ender 3 V2 3D printer to design and fabricate components using PLA filament, emphasizing precision and efficiency. Created custom 3D models for diferent projects, using Ultimaker Cura, a slicer, for optimal print settings and minimal material waste. Troubleshot and calibrated the printer to ensure high-quality output, addressing issues like bed leveling, extrusion consistency, and print temperature. Successfully completed prints for functional prototypes and aesthetic props, demonstrating proficiency in additive manufacturing, CAD modeling, and material handling.
